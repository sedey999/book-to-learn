Core Idea

Chromatic Modulation Using Enharmonically Reinterpreted Diminished-Seventh Chords Diminished-seventh chords can also be respelled for the purposes of modulating to distant keys. Each diminished-seventh chord can be respelled such that any of the its four notes is the root, and thus, can be resolved to any of four different target chords.
